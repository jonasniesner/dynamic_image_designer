Visual editor panel for building **OpenDisplay** `drawcustom` payloads in Home Assistant.

- YAML editor with validation and diagnostics
- Sketch, overlay, and HA-render preview modes
- Drag, resize, undo/redo, and element templates
- Export automation YAML for `opendisplay.drawcustom`

**Requires:** the [OpenDisplay](https://github.com/OpenDisplay-org/Home_Assistant_Integration) integration.
